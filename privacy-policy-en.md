# MindfulTimer Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-12

We (the developer of MindfulTimer) take your privacy seriously. This document explains how this app handles your data.

## 1. No Personal Data Collected

MindfulTimer **does not collect** any personally identifiable information.

There is no sign-up, no login, and we do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or any contact information.

## 2. All Data Stays on Your Device

All data generated while using the app is stored **only on your device** and is **never uploaded or synced to any remote server**. This includes, but is not limited to:

- Practice records (type, start/end time, duration, timer mode)
- Preferences (sound, bell selection, haptics, voice-note toggle, silent-switch behavior, share template, etc.)
- Optional voice notes recorded after a session (`.m4a` files, stored in the app sandbox)

Uninstalling the app removes all of the above as part of standard iOS behavior.

## 3. Microphone Usage

The microphone is only activated when you explicitly tap "Start Recording" on the practice-complete screen.

- Recordings are encoded as AAC and stored locally in the app sandbox.
- Recordings are never transmitted to any server or third party.
- You can delete any single record (which also deletes its voice file) or clear all records to remove every recording.

## 4. Sharing

The app provides a feature to share a practice summary as text. The content is composed locally and shared through the system share sheet to the destination you choose. Nothing is routed through our servers.

## 5. No Tracking Technologies

- No third-party analytics, advertising, or behavior-tracking SDKs.
- No IDFA, no device identifier collection.
- No cookies or similar technologies.

## 6. Children's Privacy

This app is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any data from them.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy because of future feature changes, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above.
